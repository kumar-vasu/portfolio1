Covid coughbinary classification

LSTM and CNN from scratch

In this project we had to classify cough sounds from patient database and predict the probability of cough being from a covid positive patient. We worked with coughvid dataset. The sound files were present in .ogg or .webm format. We converted them into .wav format for easy processing. The cough sound clips were segmented into smaller segments of indivisual coughs to increase the number of data and also to avoid processing blank or waste sound clip sections. Calculated the MFCC, delta MFCC and delta delta MFCC for feature extraction. Finally processed the data via combination of LSTM and CNN to retain the sequential relationship.
